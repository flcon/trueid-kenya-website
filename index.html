About TrueID Kenya

TrueID Kenya Limited provides nationwide biometric identity verification services designed to confirm the genuine identity of individuals involved in critical transactions such as land sales, employment, and financial dealings.

Our hybrid model combines remote coordination with a network of regional agents who collect fingerprints and ID data physically. These biometrics are submitted to authorized government authorities for official verification, ensuring authenticity and trust in every transaction.

We are registered with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) and fully comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019). Our operations align with DCI guidelines for biometric data submission, ensuring the highest standards of security and privacy.
